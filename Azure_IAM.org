** Identity and Access Management (IAM)

Identity and Access Management, IAM for short is a framework of
policies and tools to ensure the right people have access to the right
resources (such as Computers, applications etc). IAM involves not only
creating identities (username and password) but also involves creation
of roles that define the permissions and in turn assigning them to the
users based on the role.
